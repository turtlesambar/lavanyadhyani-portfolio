Tap into Black

  • Role: Game Trailer Designer, Animator, Artist
  • Duration: 4 Months
  • Team: Solo
  • Tools: After effects, Clip Studio Paint, Premiere Pro, Photoshop

Project Background

Tap into Black is a game trailer I created for LILA Games for their upcoming FPS extraction shooter, "BLACK." This was a four-month solo Graduation Project, where I handled everything from concept to final edit.
With the vast number of games released each week, it is easy for a title to become obscured within the overwhelming volume of content competing for attention. In this environment, gaining visibility serves as the critical lifeline for any new media product.
Game trailers offer a low-commitment form of marketing for potential consumers, requiring minimal effort or resources from the viewer's perspective.


Character Design

I used bold and stylized shapes to create a dynamic character design language. Primary inspiration for character was old cartoon network style mixed with anime style.

Environment Design

Since the world of BLACK is inspired by LA, I wanted to capture the beachy/warm atmosphere of the world through the environments. The Style language was finalised to be 2D.5 Parallax BG with texture variations to create areas of interest.

Storyboards

Since the Project time line was short, I worked on a very rough version of the story board and then immediately went in with solid color to have a better essence of the shots so we can get, both color script and a story board, worked on simultaneously at the same time.
